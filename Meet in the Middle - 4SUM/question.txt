Given n and target, find if there exists a,b,c,d such that
arr[a]+arr[b]+arr[c]+arr[d]=target with constraints as

n belongs [1,1000] & a,b,c,d belongs [0,n) & arr[n]<=10^9

- variant1 : indices a,b,c,d can be same i.e 0<= a,b,c,d <=n-1
- variant2 : 0<= a<b<c<d <=n-1
- variant3 : find such a quadruple
- variant4 : count all such quadruples
